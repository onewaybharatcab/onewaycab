📖 Our Story

Why We Started One-Way Bhaarat

India is a country of incredible distances. Millions travel between cities, towns and villages every day for work, family and pilgrimage. Yet outstation cab travelers faced a persistent problem — being charged for the driver's return trip even on one-way journeys.

We founded One-Way Bhaarat to fix this. Our mission: connect passengers with verified drivers for one-way intercity trips at fair, transparent prices with no hidden charges and no forced round-trip billing.

Starting from Ghaziabad, UP, we've grown one satisfied customer at a time to serve 50,000+ happy riders across 20,000+ locations. From metros to the smallest villages on Google Maps — if you need to go there, we'll get you there.

We are proudly Indian, proudly Make in India, committed to serving every corner of this great nation with dignity, safety and affordability.

🏆 Milestones

Our Journey So Far

From a small startup in Ghaziabad to India's fastest-growing one-way cab platform.

2022 — Founded
One-Way Bhaarat Launched
Started operations from Ghaziabad, UP with 10 partner drivers and 5 routes in the NCR region.
2022 — Growth
First 500 Completed Trips
Reached 500 trips milestone in 6 months. Expanded to the Delhi–Agra–Jaipur triangle.
2023 — Expansion
Pan-India Coverage Begins
Expanded to Mumbai, Bangalore, Chennai and Hyderabad. Fleet grew to 50+ driver partners.
2023 — Technology
Digital Platform Launch
Online booking website with OTP verification, fare calculator, and payment gateway integration. 5,000+ customers served.
2024 — Milestone
